**Quantum Toroidal Harmonics (QTH) Cheat Sheet**
(Exploratory geometric framework)

### Key Terminology
- **Photonic Toroid** (also called EM Toroid): A self-confined, double-loop toroidal structure made of electromagnetic energy (photons) circulating at speed *c*.
- All stable particles are understood as quantized Photonic Toroids with specific harmonic resonances and topology.

### 1. Core Building Blocks: Photonic Toroids (Williamson & van der Mark model)
All fundamental particles are interpreted as self-confined Photonic Toroids — double-loop toroidal electromagnetic energy flows in which photons are trapped at speed *c* (following the Williamson & van der Mark toroidal model).

- **Electron / Positron**: The simplest Photonic Toroid (~0.511 MeV). Opposite handedness and direction of the electric-field spine (inward = electron, outward = positron).
- **Proton**: A more complex multi-harmonic Photonic Toroid (~938 MeV) with circular polarization and superimposed resonances (especially 1/3 and 1/9). Net positive charge arises from the dominant outward E-field circulation.
- **Neutron**: A plane-polarized multi-harmonic Photonic Toroid with resonances including 1/3 + 1/9 + 1/27. Overall neutral, with an outer negative harmonic layer that enables binding.

The stability and properties of each particle emerge from the specific winding, harmonic content, and tumble of its Photonic Toroid.

### 2. Global Chiral Rotation of the EM Vacuum
A small background chiral twist **Ω_χ** (a preferred handedness in the underlying electromagnetic vacuum) biases the formation and survival of Photonic Toroids.

- Today this residual twist is extremely weak: Ω_χ ≈ 8.6 × 10^{-21} rad/s.
- It manifests observationally as **cosmic birefringence** of the CMB: β ≈ 0.215° ± 0.074° (ACT DR6 2025, 2.9σ). Combined analyses with Planck/WMAP reach ~4.7σ.

This global twist is the same mechanism proposed to seed the early matter-antimatter asymmetry and later galaxy spin handedness.

### 3. Two-Stage Matter-Antimatter Asymmetry
The observed baryon asymmetry (η_b ≈ 6 × 10^{-10}) arises naturally in two stages driven by the chiral twist:

- **Stage 1** (very early, high temperature ~150 MeV): The much stronger early chiral twist preferentially stabilizes complex proton-type Photonic Toroids over their antimatter counterparts.
- **Stage 2** (later, ~0.5 MeV): The small net excess of protons polarizes the surrounding electromagnetic vacuum, which then biases the simpler electron-type Photonic Toroids toward negative charge, automatically restoring overall charge neutrality.

### 4. Scaling with Cosmic Expansion
Both the chiral twist **Ω_χ** and the effective gravitational strength (via permittivity shifts δϵ/ε₀) dilute proportionally to baryon density as **∝ 1/a³**.

- Proton condensation epoch (a ~ 10^{-12}): Extremely strong chiral bias and gravity → matches observed baryon asymmetry.
- Electron-positron era (a ~ 10^{-10}): Still significantly enhanced bias → helps establish charge neutrality.
- Galaxy formation era (z ~ 10, a ~ 0.1): Moderate residual twist → seeds the observed ~2:1 galaxy spin handedness asymmetry (stronger at higher redshift).
- Today (a = 1): Very weak residual twist → observed CMB birefringence of ~0.215°.

### 5. Emergent Gravity (Robinson Model)
Gravity is not a fundamental force but emerges from local changes in vacuum permittivity caused by Photonic Toroids.

- Dense clusters of proton/neutron Photonic Toroids raise the local permittivity (ε_m = ε₀ + δϵ).
- This creates a radial gradient in refractive index.
- Photons and all matter (being made of confined photons) refract toward regions of higher ε → the attractive effect we call gravity.
- The exact metric is **z = e^{α/2r} – 1** (where α ∝ total number of nucleons). In the weak-field limit this recovers Newton’s law and the leading post-Newtonian corrections of General Relativity.
- Gravity reaches a maximum at r = α/2 and then weakens inside that radius → no singularities and naturally hollow toroidal cores.

### 6. Time Dilation from Early Strong Gravity
In the hot, dense early universe the much larger permittivity shifts (δϵ) created a stronger global gravitational potential. This caused significant gravitational time dilation: clocks ran much slower compared with today’s reference frame. The extended proper time available during this epoch gave the chiral twist sufficient “room” to establish the observed matter asymmetry.

The same global chiral twist that biases early particle formation also imprints a preferred handedness during structure formation, contributing to the observed large-scale galaxy spin asymmetry and coherent rotation of cosmic filaments.

### 9. Additional Phenomena in the QTH Picture
- **Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR)**: Collective precession and ringing of aligned proton Photonic Toroids in an external magnetic field.
- **Nuclear fission & fusion**: Reconfiguration of Photonic Toroid clusters into more (or less) stable harmonic packings. The mass defect corresponds to the difference in total confined electromagnetic energy before and after rearrangement. Prompt gamma rays are excess harmonic energy radiated as free photons.
- **Aneutronic fusion** (p + ¹¹B, p + ⁷Li): Clean resonant reconfiguration into stable alpha Photonic Toroids with minimal neutron production.
- **Polarization of gamma rays**: Individual photons are circularly polarized (helicity ±1). Macroscopic linear polarization arises from coherent superposition when many Photonic Toroids are aligned.
