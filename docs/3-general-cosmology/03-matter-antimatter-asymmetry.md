**Matter-Antimatter Asymmetry in Quantum Toroidal Harmonics (QTH)**

### 1. Introduction

One of the longest-standing puzzles in cosmology is the observed matter-antimatter asymmetry: why is there far more matter than antimatter in the observable universe?

The Standard Model and conventional cosmology require additional CP-violating mechanisms (Sakharov conditions) at very high energies to explain the small but non-zero baryon-to-photon ratio η_b ≈ 6 × 10^{-10}.

In the QTH framework, this asymmetry arises naturally from the **global chiral twist** in the electromagnetic vacuum through a simple two-stage process.

### 2. The Two-Stage Mechanism

The asymmetry is established in two distinct epochs, driven by the same underlying chiral twist **Ω_χ**:

**Stage 1 – Proton Bias (Very Early Universe)**  
**Stage 2 – Electron Preference & Charge Neutrality (Later Epoch)**

### 3. Stage 1: Proton Bias (T ≈ 150 MeV)

At even higher temperatures, many complex Photonic Toroids existed (as observed in collider experiments), but they were highly unstable and decayed almost immediately. The first **stable** complex Photonic Toroids — protons and neutrons — could only form once the temperature had dropped to around 150 MeV. At that point, the chiral twist was still extremely strong (∝ 1/a³).

This strong twist preferentially stabilized the more complex, multi-harmonic **proton-type Photonic Toroids** over their antimatter counterparts. The higher harmonic content of the proton toroid made it more sensitive to the background handedness.

Result: A small but significant net excess of protons (and neutrons) survived after annihilation.

### 4. Stage 2: Electron Preference & Charge Neutrality (T ≈ 0.5 MeV)

Once a small net excess of protons existed, they began to polarize the surrounding electromagnetic vacuum through their own local permittivity shifts (δε).

This proton-induced polarization created a preferred direction for the simpler **electron-type Photonic Toroids** (inward E-field spine = negative charge).

Consequently, during the later electron-positron annihilation era, electrons were slightly favored over positrons. This automatically restored overall charge neutrality without requiring fine-tuning.

### 5. Why This Works So Well in QTH

- Both stages are driven by the **same** global chiral twist Ω_χ.
- The scaling **∝ 1/a³** naturally makes Stage 1 extremely effective while Stage 2 is milder but sufficient for neutrality.
- No new particles, no high-scale CP violation, and no fine-tuned parameters are required.
- The mechanism is directly linked to the same physics that produces cosmic birefringence and galaxy spin asymmetry.

### 6. Observational Consistency

This two-stage picture quantitatively matches:
- The observed baryon asymmetry η_b ≈ 6 × 10^{-10}
- The requirement of charge neutrality in the present universe
- The timing of key epochs (proton formation before electron formation)
